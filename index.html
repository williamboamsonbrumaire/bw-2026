Frequently Asked Questions

Clear answers about how I work and what you can expect.

How is it like? What to expect?

A focused, collaborative, one-on-one technical experience. We start with a discovery phase to align on goals and strategy, then move into planning and execution with clear communication at every step.

What do you obtain?

You gain clarity, confidence, and a reliable digital solution. Beyond delivery, you also understand how the system works and how to operate or evolve it independently.

What if you change your mind in the middle of a job?

That’s completely fine. Feedback and adjustments can be integrated at any stage. Any significant scope change is discussed clearly and reflected transparently in the final cost.

How much do I charge?

Pricing depends on project scope and complexity. You’ll always know your financial commitment before we start. Flexible options are available, including subscription-based support for ongoing technical needs.
